Disruptive costumer jourey and product sizing for luxury e-commerce brand Etro, redesign of the product detail page.

Etro is an Italian fashion brand founded on 1968 as a textile desgin company, in which it's he main stylistic driver for a long time was the paisley pattern. Currently Etro is in a phase of transformation, with Marco de Vincenzo as it's new creative director. In between all this changes, one of the goals is to improve the user experience and interface in their e-commerce.

The design proposed is a disruptive, simplified product detail page (pdp) with a clean design and a thought structure to facilitate the costumer's journey. We propose a clear separation between the e-commerce product, and the prodcut information. In addition, we integrate an easier to understand sizing system to reduce the returns on the products, since we notice is one of the main reasons of returns.
