Helif is a website build to act as a host for users to access quality content blogs with ease, all on one platform.

Helif is a website which plans to act as a host for users to access quality blogs about a variety of topics, specifically the categories lifestyle, crafts and cooking all on one platform. It is giving users a blogging community experience which will allow them to search, follow and save posts from their favourite bloggers and access quality content blogs worldwide. The main concept behind is to be sort of a social media platform for blogging.

It's unique selling point (USP) aims to be a hassle free, modern, and inclusive way to browse your favourite blogs in one spot, reduce website hopping and follow your favourite creators with ease.

The biggest challenge is to design an effective, clear and minimal website, able to hold big amount of visual and written information, so the user is not only able to easily navigate the website, but also to enjoy it.
