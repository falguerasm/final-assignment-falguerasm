Redesign of the graphic identity of Poblenou Eix Comercial. New identity proposal based on Poblenou heritage and its people. We have created a graphic universe blending tradition and innovation.

The main objective of this visual identity is to represent the essence of Poblenou commerce, looking for the graphic to pay homage only to the Poblenou neighborhood. That is why we take as a starting point old graphic documents and commercial advertisements of Poblenou and analyze them in the formal field, avoiding falling into nostalgia and vintage graphics.

To continue with the idea of tribute mentioned above, we decided to recover an old claim from a shopkeepers’ guild campaign, prior to the 22@ plan and the wild gentrification that Poblenou has suffered as a result: Embotiga't

Embotiga't is a subjective—shop—turned into an imperative verb. It is almost an invitation, an order. It is this logic that gives rise to the rest of the words that make up the language of the Axis. All of them are verbs that have to do with participating in local commerce: Eat. River. Open. Close. hello Touch. Walk... Embotiga't
