ASMR is a conceptual book cover that represents in a visual and simplified way what the concept of asmr is, in addition to simulating the reaction of it in ourselves.

ASMR n.: 1. Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response (ASMR) is a sensory phenomenon in which people experience a static tingling sensation across the scalp, back of the neck, and sometimes other areas in response to specific visual and sound stimuli.

This interactive cover explores and conceptualizes the idea of ASMR, to visually represent it as a book cover. It represents the physical reaction certain people experience when faced with a stimulus, whether audio or visual. The stimuli (represented by the magnetized sidebar), awaken and activates the hair, (iron threads). The irregular upper container symbolize the epidermis itself.
