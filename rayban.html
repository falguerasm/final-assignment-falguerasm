Ray-Ban Stories phone website version redesign to a simplified and cleaner design, based on user experience research.

Ray-Ban Stories, is the latest innovation from the company, a collaboration with Facebook META. This smart eyeglasses/sunglasses, come with camera and audio included on it, combining META's technology with Ray-Ban's style. With these glasses one can take photos or listen to music just by tapping on the glasses. These glasses are so unique that there's a lot of characteristics to be told and informec about, that might be a bit overwhelming.

This new design proposal aims to ease the information displayed by just simplifiying the design, but without loosing the art direction of the product. Based on user testing research the new design is less crowded, more minimal, but keeping the look and feel of the product.

Contributors: Chiara Bonzano, Andres Ossa, Virginia Pastore and Elisa Orlandi
