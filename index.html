Hola! So nice to meet you, let me introduce a bit of myself

→ about me

I’m Mia, a graphic and digital designer from Barcelona. I am an enthusiasthic visual designer, curious learner and a great graphic assets collector (I love to collect business cards mainly). I love working and learning from others as well as enjoying time by my self. Aside from design my other passion is travel, and learn from other people and cultures.

→ my work

My work revolves around a strong communication and a solid grid based design. My visual approach aims to be straigth forward, content focused and well-thought. Embracing the use of distinguished typography and the absence of color to empower contrast and simplicity.
