Year:

2022

Esconderse a plena vista (en: Hiding in Plain Sight) is the final-degree thesis based on research and experimentation, which seeks to understand facial recognition systems, and develop artistic strategies to avoid being detected.

Artificial intelligence and facial recognition systems are real and they are here. We live in a society, which Deleuze calls Societies of Control, in which security is valued over privacy, and we find these biometric systems in security agencies, social networks, etc. Facial recognition systems are opaque and do not allow us to understand what is happening inside the machines, while they observe and analyse our faces on a daily basis, beyond our control.

To combat this problem, and the human need for privacy, there are artistic and design strategies created to hide or transfigure the face. This research takes place in this context, designing “masks” that manage to evade detection and presenting them in the form of a video-essay on the subject.
