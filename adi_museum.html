Tribute to Luceplan's award-winning Compasso d'Oro designs, stressing it's methodology and view on design.

The ADI Design Museum Compasso d’Oro is one of the largest museums in Europe dedicated to design. It possesses the full collection of Compasso d’Oro's award-winning products and projects since the begining, the most prestigious Italian design accolade. Its aim is to highlight the quality of Made in Italy products.

Luceplan is a former award-winning lamp designers, with no more than 6 awards. Their design method based on a refined process of research and innovation in the world of light, balancing functionallity and aesthetics is what makes them so unique.

Therefore, this collection of posters is a tribute to their work, their design and their methodology. The concept behind is to embrace the power of light by playing with darkness and light itself.
